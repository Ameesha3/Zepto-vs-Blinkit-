# Grocery Store Analysis Dashboard: Zepto vs Blinkit

 Project Description
This project is a **comparative analytics dashboard** built in **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze and compare the performance of two leading grocery delivery platforms – **Zepto** and **Blinkit**.  
The dashboard provides insights into **sales, delivery efficiency, customer ratings, payment preferences, and product category performance** across multiple cities.  


 Tech Stack
- **Power BI Desktop** – Data modeling, DAX measures, and interactive visuals  
- **Excel Dataset (Zepto vs Blinkit For Power BI.xlsx)** – Transaction-level grocery sales data  
- **MS Excel** – Data cleaning and transformation  
- **Visualization Techniques** – KPI cards, bar charts, line charts, donut charts, and maps  

Data Source
- **Zepto vs Blinkit Dataset** – Contains order-level details:  
  - Brand (Zepto / Blinkit)  
  - Date of Order  
  - Product & Category (Milk, Snacks, Rice, Biscuits, etc.)  
  - City (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai)  
  - Price & Quantity  
  - Delivery Time (minutes)  
  - Payment Mode (UPI, Card, COD)  
  - Customer Rating (1–5 scale)  

 Features & Highlights

 Business Problem
Grocery delivery companies face challenges in **tracking sales performance, delivery efficiency, and customer satisfaction** across multiple cities and product categories.  
Decision-makers need a comparative view to identify **strengths and weaknesses of each brand**.

 Goal of Dashboard
- Compare **Zepto vs Blinkit** on key metrics (Sales, Delivery Time, Ratings)  
- Analyze **payment mode preferences** across customers  
- Identify **top-performing product categories**  
- Track **monthly sales trends**  
- Understand **city-wise performance**  

 Walkthrough of Key Visuals
- **KPI Cards** – Total Sales, Average Delivery Time, Average Rating  
- **Pie Chart** – Payment Mode Distribution (UPI, Card, COD)  
- **Bar Chart** – Monthly Sales Trend (Jan–Dec comparison)  
- **Bar Chart** – Product Category Sales (Snacks, Milk, Paneer, Rice, Biscuits, etc.)  
- **Line Chart** – City-wise Sales (Chennai, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Delhi)  
- **Brand Filter** – Compare Zepto vs Blinkit across all visuals  

Business Impact & Insights
- **Sales Performance**: Zepto and Blinkit show seasonal peaks (June–July highest sales).  
- **Delivery Efficiency**: Zepto averages faster delivery times (~15 mins) compared to Blinkit in some cities.  
- **Customer Ratings**: Average rating is ~3, highlighting scope for service improvement.  
- **Payment Preferences**: UPI dominates (36%), followed by Card (33%) and COD (31%).  
- **Category Insights**: Snacks, Milk, Paneer, and Rice are the top-selling categories.  
- **City Insights**: Chennai leads in sales, followed by Mumbai and Bangalore.  

 Business Value
This dashboard enables:
- **Competitive benchmarking** between Zepto and Blinkit  
- **Operational efficiency tracking** (delivery times, ratings)  
- **Customer behavior analysis** (payment modes, product preferences)  
- **Strategic decision-making** for marketing and product focus  


Future Enhancements
- Add **forecasting models** for monthly sales prediction  
- Include **customer segmentation** (loyal vs new customers)  
- Build **profitability analysis** by category and city  
- Integrate **real-time API data** for live monitoring
